WHY?

"Master of Magic, Worlds, and Wonder.

Brandon Sanderson is a towering figure in the realms of fantasy and science fiction literature. Known for his intricate world-building and innovative magic systems, Sanderson has captivated readers worldwide with his expansive Cosmere universe, home to critically acclaimed series such as Mistborn and The Stormlight Archive.
His prowess extends beyond the Cosmere. He has penned young adult series like The Reckoners and Skyward, and completed Robert Jordan's legendary The Wheel of Time series, showcasing his versatility and depth as a storyteller. Sanderson's contributions to the genre are not limited to novels alone; he has ventured into graphic novels with works like White Sand and Dark One, further solidifying his status as a creative powerhouse.
