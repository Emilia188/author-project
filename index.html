WHY?

"Master of Magic, Worlds, and Wonder.

Brandon Sanderson is a towering figure in the realms of fantasy and science fiction literature. Known for his intricate world-building and innovative magic systems, Sanderson has captivated readers worldwide with his expansive Cosmere universe, home to critically acclaimed series such as Mistborn and The Stormlight Archive.
His prowess extends beyond the Cosmere. He has penned young adult series like The Reckoners and Skyward, and completed Robert Jordan's legendary The Wheel of Time series, showcasing his versatility and depth as a storyteller. Sanderson's contributions to the genre are not limited to novels alone; he has ventured into graphic novels with works like White Sand and Dark One, further solidifying his status as a creative powerhouse.

BOOKS

My favorite books by Brandon Sanderson

The Way of Kings

An epic introduction to Brandon Sanderson's The Stormlight Archive, showcasing his masterful world-building and complex, interwoven plotlines. The book sets the stage with memorable characters, intricate magic systems, and a richly detailed world, making it a compelling and immersive start to an ambitious fantasy series.

Published

August 31, 2010

What I like about?

If you’re intimidated by this book/series, don’t be. It’s a vast world but I assure you that Sanderson has got your back and will hold your hand as he leads the way to a world that’s just brimming with imagination.

Buy a book

The Final Empire

The first book in Mistborn series, is a gripping and inventive fantasy that introduces a unique magic system based on metal consumption. With its thrilling heist plot, richly crafted world, and themes of rebellion and trust, it offers a fresh take on the traditional "chosen one" trope, while also delivering deep character arcs and an unpredictable, engaging story.

Published

July 17, 2006

What I like about?

The main plot of the book revolves around a rebellion to overthrow the lord ruler; this rebellion is built around a heist led by Kelsier, his team, and his newly found apprentice—Vin.

Buy a book

Words of Radiance

Words of Radiance is a brilliant continuation of The Stormlight Archive, building on the foundation laid in The Way of Kings with deeper exploration of its world, characters, and magic systems. It focuses heavily on the growth of Shallan and Kaladin, delving into their backstories, struggles, and internal conflicts, making them even more compelling.

Published

March 4, 2014

What I like about?

Its exploration of themes like personal growth, loyalty, and moral complexity adds richness to the story, keeping readers engaged throughout its dense yet rewarding narrative.
